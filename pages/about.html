ABOUT ME

I live in the Salt Lake area and enjoy going on hiking and camping adventures with my family, consisting of my wife, four daughters, and our shitzu-poodle Pepper. My four daughters and I have hiked over 25 different peaks together and want to capture the views that we witness on our many hikes.

I am not a professional photographer, but I enjoy taking photos and have taught myself this skill along with photo editing. Other hobbies include rock houding, hiking, running, watching movies with my family, and spending time in nature.

Some of my favorite spots:

  • Goblin Valley State Park: Emery County, Utah
  • Twin Peaks: Wasatch Mountains
